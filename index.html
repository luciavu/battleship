Battleship is a discovery game in which players need to discover their opponent's ship positions.
Each player secretly arranges their ships on their grid, either horizontically or vertically. The number of squares taken up by each ship is determined by their type. Both players have the same number and type of ships:
No. Type Length
1x Battleship 4
2x Submarine 3
3x Patrol Boat 2
After ships have been positioned, players will take turns to target a square on the opponent's board to attack. If you land an attack, you can go again.
  • a 'hit' is marked by an X
  • a 'miss' is marked by an o
If all of a player's ships have been sunk, the game is over and their opponent wins.
